Wow! That's Engineering

Every year we look forward to hosting WOW! That’s Engineering—an all day event that includes workshops, panels, speakers, and hands-on activities with a focus on tech and engineering in order to encourage female youth to pursue careers in STEM. Last year we had over 140 attendees (highschoolers and some parents) from all over the Bay Area. Companies like Blach Construction, IBM, NASA, Lockheed Martin, and Intel held interactive workshops which incorporated building bridges, robots, code, circuits, and career-related skills
