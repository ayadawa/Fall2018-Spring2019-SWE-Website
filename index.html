Social Events

Leadership

Our annual Spring outreach event that exposes high school girls to the engineering field though hands-on workshops and keynotes led by established female engineers. We hope that our young attendees leave feeling inspired with a fresh perspective about the forefront of the tech industry.

WOW! That's Engineering

Our annual Fall networking event that comprises of recruiters from a diverse pool of engineering companies. Students and hiring managers are sectioned into small groups and converse over dinner.

Evening with Industry

Our annual Spring social event where SWE officers and members are able to connect and graduating senior executives give farewell speeches before they move on in the next chapter of their lives. All our members and SWE partners are invited to join us in this one last SWE celebration of the year.
